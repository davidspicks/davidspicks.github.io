FRI-SUN Find the latest in kitchen, bath, garden and decor. At the Washington State Fair and Events Center in Puyallup. Map

SAT Traditional Japanese New Years celebration with making and eating the sweet rice treat mochi. With kid’s crafts and activities. At the Woodward Middle School on Bainbridge Island. Map Ferry Schedule

SAT Write and debug scripts, learn basic coding rules and jargon, and use simple game theory to create one of the most famous arcade games of all time: Pac-Man. Included with admission to the Living Computers Museum + Labs. Map
